The Space

Our space was designed to be relaxing and
comforting. Each detail has been thoughtfully
considered in order to make our guests feel at
home. The layout and decor of our rooms differ
but all share the same cozy and restorative vibes.

Amenities

You deserve to feel taken care of during
your stay. We've stocked the room with all
of the essentials including toiletry kits and
local coffee. From an in-hotel restaurant to
a heated pool, you'll have everything you
need to enjoy yourself.
